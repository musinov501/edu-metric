# Judge demo flow

The canonical 7-step demo. Total time target: 4–5 minutes.

## 1. Problem framing (30s)
> "Current scholarship systems mostly rely only on GPA. EduMetric evaluates the full student ecosystem transparently — GPA, attendance, achievements, tutor feedback, discipline, employment growth — and always explains *why* a student got their score."

## 2. Admin Dashboard (45s)
Open `/admin/overview`. Walk through the top cards:
- Total students
- Eligible students
- At-risk students
- Pending approvals
- Active penalties

Show the analytics row: scholarship distribution chart, attendance trend, faculty performance.

## 3. Student Profile (60s)
Open a specific student. Highlight:
- KPI breakdown card (Academic 34/40, Attendance 18/20, Projects 13/15, etc.)
- Activity timeline
- Strengths / weaknesses
- Scholarship status badge

## 4. Achievement Upload (30s)
Switch to student account. Upload a certificate via drag-and-drop. Status: **Pending Review**.

## 5. Admin Approval (THE WOW MOMENT, 45s)
Back to admin. Open Pending Approvals panel. Click approve. Watch:
1. Score updates instantly
2. Leaderboard rank shifts
3. Notification appears
4. Activity log writes a new row

## 6. AI Insights (30s)
Open the AI panel:
- "Your attendance dropped 8%"
- "You need 4 more points for scholarship eligibility"
- "Your strongest area is project performance"

## 7. Transparency / Explainable Ranking (45s)
Hover any leaderboard entry. Show the breakdown:
```
+ Strong GPA
+ Excellent attendance
- Minor penalties
+ Strong project work
```

> "This is what makes EduMetric different. Every ranking, every score, every change — explained."

## Closing (15s)
> "AI-powered, transparent scholarship evaluation. Built to scale across universities in Uzbekistan."

## Language to use
- "transparent scholarship evaluation"
- "student growth intelligence"
- "explainable ranking system"
- "institutional-grade evaluation platform"

## Language to avoid
- "student CRM"
- "student dashboard"
- "admin panel"
